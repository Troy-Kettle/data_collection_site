Part 1: Vital Signs Concern Level Boundaries

Participant instructions:

For each of the vital signs indicated below, please use the slider arrows to indicate which values you would consider to fit into the following categories, according to your own clinical judgement.

Please assume that any abnormalities are new and have not yet been treated.

  1. No concern (green) – no requirement for urgent medical assessment.
  2. Mild concern (yellow) – may require urgent medical assessment under some circumstances.
  3. Moderate concern (orange) – requires urgent medical assessment within 1-4 hours.
  4. Severe concern (red) – requires immediate medical attention.
