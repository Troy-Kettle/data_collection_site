Part 3: Further Interpretation of Vital Signs

Participant Instructions / Task:

For each vital sign out of heart rate, systolic blood pressure, respiratory rate, temperature, oxygen saturations, and inspired oxygen requirement, participants will be asked:

  • When interpreting (insert vital sign), how important is it to take account of:
    • What is normal for that individual patient.
    • Whether the general trend is worsening or improving.
    • Abnormal values that have occurred within the past 24 hours.
