Part 3: Further Interpretation of Vital Signs

Participant Instructions / Task:

This section is about interpreting changes in vital signs over time. Please look at the following sets of vital signs, in each case showing a timeline over a period of 72 hours. Please evaluate your level of concern at the current time point, on a scale from 0 to 15.


0 = No concern – no requirement for medical assessment.

5 = Mild concern – may require medical assessment under some circumstances.

10 = Moderate concern – requires medical assessment within 1-4 hours.

15 = Severe concern – requires immediate medical attention.
